ABOUT

Hi! I'm Jauarius.

Founder

I'm a freelance photographer based in Singapore, who specializes in photographing pets. Fun fact: I have a mild pet obsession, but I don't own any pets unfortunately. I found myself taking up an offer to photograph a pet for a client I had met while studying. What started by chance turned out to be the perfect mixture of challenges and a creative outlet, and I decided right there and then that this is what I want to pursue as a career. In 2015, I founded this photography studio, Januarius Photography, to help pet owners create new memories with their pets and immortalise these moments.

Mission

Our photographers are highly experienced, and through their proficient grasp on photography techniques, they are able to capture the attributes and unique personalities of your pets. Your pet's comfort and well-being are our absolute and number one priority, and we aim to capture the very best moments. Cliche as it may be, a picture speaks a thousand words and we want to immortalise memories that you share with your pets, so that you will have a picture that you can treasure forever.
